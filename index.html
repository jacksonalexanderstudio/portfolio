Jack's Garden

An ever evolving meditation on life and growth
through the lens of a simple backyard garden
in Santa Fe — flowers, vegetables, native species,
insects, dirt, water and patience all working together
in perfect harmony. This one remains a lifelong
case study and the archive serves as a helpful
reminder of seasons past.
